FAQ

  • 1. What is an NFT?

    NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token, which is a digital asset that represents ownership of a unique item or piece of content. NFTs are often used to represent digital art, collectibles, and other unique digital assets.

  • 2. How can I buy an NFT on your site?

    To buy an NFT on this web app, you'll need to connect your Metamask wallet, and then browse the available NFTs for sale. Once you find an NFT you want to buy, you can buy it using cryptocurrency.

  • 3. What types of cryptocurrency are accepted on this web app?

    This web app currently accepts only Ethereum (ETH)

  • 4. Can I sell my own NFTs on this web app?

    Yes, you can upload the NFT and then sell it to another person as long as you have connected your Metamask wallet.

Connecting to our Application

1. Download Metamask extension for your browser

2. Create a Metamask Wallet

3. Deposit some ETH to your wallet

4. Enjoy using our web application :)

Buying NFTs

1. Connect to our site using Metamask

2. Select an NFT you want to buy from the Canvas, that isn't previously bought from someone else

3. Click on the NFT and click Buy

4. Enjoy your new NFT

Selling your NFTs Artwork

1. Connect to our site using Metamask

2. Select an artwork you want to sell

3. Fill in the Input Fields and Upload the NFT Artwork Image

4. Your artwork should be up on canvas and ready to be sold to another person
